Enterprise AI Compliance Platform - SNP Natuurreizen

🔥 In Production

Architected and developed a sophisticated AI-powered compliance platform with microservices architecture, implementing real-time GDPR and AI Act regulatory analysis. Built a complete document processing pipeline featuring semantic vector search (ChromaDB + SentenceTransformers), automated risk assessment, and multimedia report generation. The system combines JWT authentication, dual-database architecture (SQLite + ChromaDB), and intelligent document parsing across PDF, Office, and image formats with OCR. Achieved 68% test coverage with 780 passing tests, demonstrating enterprise-grade quality assurance. The platform performs automated prohibited systems detection, high-risk AI classification, and generates actionable compliance recommendations with visual analytics (matplotlib) and animated summaries (imageio).
